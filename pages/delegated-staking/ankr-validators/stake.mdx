# Stake with Ankr's validators

You may stake with our validators on a variety of different chains:
* Avalanche
* Binance
* Celer
* Fantom
* MAP
* Oasis
* Polygon
* Scale

## Avalanche

To unstake with Ankr Labs' validator on Avalanche, follow [these instructions](https://www.blockmeadow.com/how-to-stake-avax-with-validator/) if you want to stake with Avalanche wallet or [these instructions](https://coinmarketcap.com/academy/article/how-to-stake-avalanche-avax) if you want to stake with Core wallet.
