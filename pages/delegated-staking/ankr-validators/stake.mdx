# Stake with Ankr's validators

You may stake with our validators on a variety of different chains:
* Avalanche
* Binance
* Celer
* Fantom
* MAP
* Oasis
* Polygon
* Skale

## Avalanche
To unstake with the Ankr validators on Avalanche, follow [these instructions](https://www.blockmeadow.com/how-to-stake-avax-with-validator/) if you want to stake with Avalanche wallet or [these instructions](https://coinmarketcap.com/academy/article/how-to-stake-avalanche-avax) if you want to stake with Core wallet.

## Binance
To stake with the Ankr validator on Binance Smart Chain, read [this guide by Figment](https://figment.io/insights/bnb-staking-guide/) and [go to Ankr Labs' validator page on Binance](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/staking/validator/bva1xnudjls7x4p48qrk0j247htt7rl2k2dzp3mr3j).

## Celer
To stake with the Ankr validator on Celer, follow the [Celer official guide](https://cbridge-docs.celer.network/tutorial/sgn-v2-staking-guide) and find the Ankr validator on the [Celer SGN Staking page](https://sgn.celer.network/#/staking).

## Fantom
To stake with the Ankr validator on Fanrom, follow the [Fantom official guide](https://docs.fantom.foundation/staking/how-to-stake-on-fantom#guide) and find the Ankr validator on the Fantom's [fWallet Staking page](https://wallet.fantom.network/staking).

## Mapo
To stake with the Ankr validator on Mapo, follow the [Mapo official guide](https://www.mapprotocol.io/blog/map-token-staking/) and find the Ankr validator on the [Mapo's Staking page](https://staking.mapprotocol.io/). Mapo also features a similar user guide as part of their [staking landing page](https://www.mapprotocol.io/stake-mapo).

## Oasis
To stake with the Ankr validators on Oasis, we suggest you use the [Oasis Web Wallet](https://wallet.oasis.io/)  and follow the [Staking Guide on Oasis blog](https://oasisprotocol.org/blog/how-to-stake-the-oasis-rose-token), which sums up to:
1. Simply navigate to the **Staking** or **Escrow** sections, and select a validator from the list.
2. Delegate the preferred amount of ROSE tokens to one of the two Ankr validators.
3. Potentially specify the gas price for the transaction.
4. Confirm the staking transaction in your wallet.

For more information, read the [official wallet creation guide](https://docs.oasis.io/general/manage-tokens/oasis-wallets/web/) and discover [more tools](https://docs.oasis.io/general/manage-tokens/staking-and-delegating/#tools-to-use-for-staking-and-delegation) to use for staking and delegation.

## Polygon
To stake with the Ankr validator on Polygon, follow [this staking guide](https://www.cryptovantage.com/guides/how-to-stake-polygon-matic/) and find the Ankr Validator on the [Polygon Staking page](https://staking.polygon.technology/).

## Skale
To stake with the Ankr validators on Skale, follow the [official delegation guide](https://docs.skale.network/delegators/).
