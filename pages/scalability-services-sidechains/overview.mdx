# Sidechains as a service

What is Sidechains as a service?
Sidechains as a service is a scalability service offered by Ankr to large and small businesses.

Sidechains as a service allows you to have your own blockchain network with custom rules and parameters.
The great reputation of the leading Web3 infrastructure company guarantees that quality and involvement of Ankr engineers collecting initial parameters, setting up a sidechain, and supporting streamlining it into your company's daily routine.

## What is a sidechain?
A sidechain is an independent EVM-compatible blockchain which runs in parallel to Mainnet.
Sidechains are compatible with the Mainnet via two-way bridges, and run under their own chosen rules of consensus, and block parameters.
