# Overview

*RPC Service* — a platform that enables users to empower their Web3 projects with the blockchain interaction capabilities ([35+ chains supported](/rpc-service/chains/chains-list/)) via the top-performing globally-distributed decentralized infrastructure of nodes.
<br/>

<img src="/docs/build/rpc-overview.png" alt="RPC Overview" class="responsive-pic" width="800" />

The **Getting Started** docs walk you through the following items:

  * [RPC Service interface schema](/rpc-service/getting-started/overview/#interface-schema) — get to know the RPC Service interface.
  * [Public plan basics](/rpc-service/getting-started/basics-public/) — get to know your way around Public endpoints for blockchain interaction.
  * [Premium plan basics](/rpc-service/getting-started/basics-premium/) — get to know your way around Premium endpoints for blockchain interaction.
  * [Business account](/rpc-service/getting-started/my-business/) — get to know your way around user accounts that help structuring your business.
