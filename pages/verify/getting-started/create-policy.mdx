# Create a policy as a vendor
<Callout type="tip">
To become a vendor, [talk to our experts](https://meetings-eu1.hubspot.com/arsalan/).
</Callout>
As a vendor, you can create different policies for the users of your Web3 app to comply with.
1. Visit your [Ankr Verify vendor account](https://ankr.com/verify/vendor/).
2. Click **Connect MetaMask** and proceed to connect the address that has been approved by Ankr as a vendor address.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/click-connect-metamask-vendor.jpg" alt="Click Connect MetaMask" class="responsive-pic" width="800" />
3. Click **Add Policy**.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/click-add-policy.jpg" alt="Click Add Policy" class="responsive-pic" width="800" />
4. Set a **Policy Name** to recognize it later. The set the **Attribute type** to check, a **Condition**, and the attribute's **Value**. And write a telling **Requirement Description** to show to your users.
   To add more than one requirement to the policy, click **Add Entry Requirement** and repeat the process. When done, click **Add Policy**.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/customize-policy.jpg" alt="Set up your policy" class="responsive-pic" width="500" />
5. If you wish to customize your policy a bit more, click **No, check again**. If you are ready to add the policy, click **Yes, add policy**.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/click-yes-to-finalize-adding-policy.jpg" alt="Click Yes to finish adding or No to return to editing" class="responsive-pic" width="500" />
6. On the success page, click **View all policies** to return to the policy list.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/policy-successfuly-created.jpg" alt="Click View all policies" class="responsive-pic" width="500" />
7. Now you can see the policies users of your Web3 app need to comply with.
   <img src="/docs/verify/getting-started/list-of-added-policies.jpg" alt="List of added policies" class="responsive-pic" width="800" />
