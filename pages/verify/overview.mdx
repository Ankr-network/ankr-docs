# Ankr Verify

AnkrVerify is an identity verification service for Web3 that upgrades the convenience of Single Sign-On to the blockchain level.

Now you can avoid vulnerabilities of providing your sensitive password, credit card, ID, and other personal data to every Web3 app that asks you for it.
You can create your digital ID and meet the requirements of blockchains and dApps without ever showing your information, thanks to zero-knowledge cryptography.
