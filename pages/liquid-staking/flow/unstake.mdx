# Unstake FLOW

## Unstake options

Currently, the only option is **Standard unstake**. It takes 7–15 days before you receive the funds. No commission or limits imposed.

## Unstake FLOW

### Standard unstake

1. Open [Ankr Staking Dashboard](https://www.ankr.com/staking/dashboard/). 
2. In the **ankrFLOW** tile, click the **—** button to unstake.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/flow/click-minus.png" className="responsive-pic" width="600" />
3. In the **FLOW Liquid Staking** dialog, on the **Unstake** tab, enter the amount, and then click **Unstake**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/flow/unstake-amount.png" className="responsive-pic" width="400" />
4. Confirm your unstake transaction in MetaMask.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/flow/confirm-unstake.png" className="responsive-pic" width="250" />
5. In the next **Unstake in progress** dialog, click **Go to dashboard**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/flow/unstake-in-progress.png" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />

<Callout type="success">
Once transaction is confirmed, Ankr Staking Dashboard updates to show **Unstaking in progress** in the token tile.<br/>
</Callout>
