# Stake SUI

## Before you start
Ensure the following:
* You are using a Chrome Browser.
* You have the [Sui wallet extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sui-wallet/opcgpfmipidbgpenhmajoajpbobppdil) installed and set up in Chrome.

## Get SUI and send it to Sui Wallet
1. Create a SUI address using [Sui wallet](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sui-wallet/opcgpfmipidbgpenhmajoajpbobppdil).
2. Purchase SUI at [https://www.binance.com/](https://www.binance.com) or other exchanges.

## Connect your wallet
To connnect your Sui Wallet, first you need to connect any Ethereum-compatible wallet like MetaMask.
### Step one: connect an Ethereum-compatible wallet
1. Visit [Ankr Staking Dashboard](https://www.ankr.com/staking/dashboard/).
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/ankr-staking.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="600" />
2. Click **Connect wallet** in the top-right corner of the page.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/connect-wallet-button.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="140" />
3. Choose **MetaMask**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/choose-wallet-to-connect.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />
4. If you have multiple accounts in MetaMask, choose an account to connect and click **Next**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/choose-account-to-connect.jpg" alt="Choose an account" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />
5. Click **Connect** and wait for a couple of moments till the connecting process is finished.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/connect-account.jpg" alt="Connect the account" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />
6. Successful connection to **Ankr Staking** is indicated at top right corner by the address of wallet you connected.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/wallet-connected.jpg" alt="Wallet connected" className="responsive-pic" width="220" />
7. If asked by Ankr Staking to switch networks, select a network of your choice.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/select-supported-network.jpg" alt="Select a supported network" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />
8. Click **Switch network** to confirm switching.
   <img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/confirm-switching-networks-ethereum.jpg" alt="Confirm switching networks" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />

### Step two: connect Sui Wallet
1. Visit [Ankr Staking Dashboard](https://www.ankr.com/staking/dashboard/).
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/ankr-staking-sui.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="600" />
2. Click the **+** button in the top-right corner of the page.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/connect-wallet-button-sui.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="600" />
3. Choose **Sui Wallet**.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/choose-wallet-to-connect-sui.jpg" alt="Connect wallet button" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />
4. If you have multiple accounts in Sui Wallet, choose an account and click **Connect**.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/choose-account-to-connect-sui.jpg" alt="Choose an account and click Connect" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />
6. Successful connection to **Ankr Staking** is indicated at top right corner by the address of wallet you connected.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/wallet-connected-sui.jpg" alt="Wallet connected" className="responsive-pic" width="220" />
7. If asked by Ankr Staking to switch networks, select a network of your choice.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/select-supported-network.jpg" alt="Select a supported network" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />
8. Click **Switch network** to confirm switching.
<img src="/docs/staking/connect-wallet/confirm-switching-networks-ethereum.jpg" alt="Confirm switching networks" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />

## Stake SUI
<Callout type="info">
Ankr auto-deducts a 10% technical service fee from your staking reward for the provided services and operations, each reward cycle.<br/>
The minimum amount to stake is 0.1 SUI.
</Callout>
1. Visit [Ankr Staking](https://www.ankr.com/staking/stake).
2. Locate the **Sui (SUI)** box and click **Stake** in it.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/sui-staking-box.jpg" alt="Click Stake in Sui (SUI) box" className="responsive-pic" width="700" />
4. Enter the desired amount of SUI to stake and click or **Get ankrSUI**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/enter-amount-click-get.jpg" alt="Enter an amount and click Get" className="responsive-pic" width="500" />
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/confirm-staking-transaction.jpg" alt="Click Approve" className="responsive-pic" width="300" />
7. Click **Go to dashboard** to see the added token. You may need to wait a little for the transaction to finalize and **Dashboard** to automatically update. The token is also auto-added to your Sui Wallet.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/click-go-to-dashboard.jpg" alt="Click Go to dashboard" className="responsive-pic" width="550" />

<Callout type="success">
To reflect your staking rewards, your ankrSUI keeps growing in price to SUI, never changing in number.<br/>
To get more yield out of your Liquid Staking tokens, visit [Ankr DeFi](https://ankr.com/staking/defi/) (coming soon).
</Callout>
