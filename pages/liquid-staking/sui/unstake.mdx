# Unstake SUI
<Callout type="info">
Ensure you have a small amount of SUI to pay the Sui gas fee for your unstaking transaction.<br/>
    The minimum amount to unstake is 0.5 SUI.
</Callout>

1. Open [Ankr Staking Dashboard](https://www.ankr.com/staking/dashboard/). 
2. Locate the ankrSUI box and click the **-** icon to unstake.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/click-minus-icon.jpg" alt="Click the minus icon" class="responsive-pic" width="800" />
3. Enter the desired amount, and click **Unstake**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/enter-amount-click-unstake.jpg" alt="Enter an amount and click Approve" class="responsive-pic" width="400" />
6. Confirm the unstaking transaction in Sui Wallet.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/confirm-unstaking-transaction.jpg" alt="Confirm transaction in Sui Wallet" class="responsive-pic" width="300" />
7. On the next **Unstake successful!** page, click **Go to dashboard**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-staking/sui/unstake-successful.jpg" alt="Confirm transaction in Sui Wallet" class="responsive-pic" width="500" />

<Callout type="success">
    The unstaked SUI will be released to your wallet in up to 1 day.
</Callout>
