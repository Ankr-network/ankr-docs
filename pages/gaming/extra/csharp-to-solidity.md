# C# and Solidity types comparison

Here is the comparison of types in C# and Solidity:

| C#           | Solidity                       |
|--------------|--------------------------------|
| `string`     | `address`                      |
| `BigInteger` | `uint8` to `uint256`           |
| `byte[]`     | `bytes`, `bytes1` to `bytes32` |
| `bool`       | `bool`                         |
| `string`     | `string`                       |
