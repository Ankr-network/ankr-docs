# Unstake a Liquid Crowdloan

## Before you start
Ensure the following:
* You are using a Chrome Browser.
* You have a [MetaMask wallet extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn) installed and set up in Chrome.
* You have a [Polkadot.js wallet extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd) installed and set up in Chrome.

## Connect MetaMask
To unstake, you will need both MetaMask and Polkadot.js wallets.

Let's connect MetaMask first:
1. Switch to Ethereum Mainnet in MetaMask.
2. Visit the [Liquid Crowdloan page](https://www.ankr.com/staking/liquid-crowdloan/dot/) in Ankr Staking.
3. Click the MetaMask box in the wallet connection popup and proceed to connect your wallet.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-metamask-to-connect.jpg" alt="Click MetaMask" class="responsive-pic" width="450" />
   <Callout type="tip">
    If after you've connected MetaMask, you see the "Failed to get rewards data" message, switch to Ethereum Mainnet in MetaMask, reload the page, and connect your wallet again.
   </Callout>

## Connect Polkadot.js
To unstake, you will need both MetaMask and Polkadot.js wallets.

Once you have connected [MetMask](#connect-metamask), you can connect Polkadot.js:
1. Visit the [Liquid Crowdloan page](https://www.ankr.com/staking/liquid-crowdloan/dot/) in Ankr Staking.
2. Click **Connect wallet**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-connect-wallet-to-conect-polkadot-js.jpg" alt="Click Connect wallet" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />
3. Choose **Polkadot.js** and proceed to connect it.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-and-connect-polkadot.jpg" alt="Click Polkadot.js Wallet" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />

## Claim rewards
If you unstake (redeem) before claiming your rewards, you will lose them.

Let's claim your rewards first:
1. Having connected your wallets, check your projects at the [Liquid Crowdloan page](https://www.ankr.com/staking/liquid-crowdloan/dot/).
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/list-of-projects.jpg" alt="Check projects for unclaimed rewards" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />
2. Click **Claim** next to a project with unclaimed rewards.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-claim.jpg" alt="Click Claim" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />
   <Callout type="tip">
   If you see a popup with the Clover and Polkadot.js wallets, [connect Polkadot.js](#connect-polkadotjs).
   </Callout>
4. Enter a Polkadot-compatible address and click **Continue**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/enter-withdrawal-add-click-continue.jpg" alt="Enter address and click continue" class="responsive-pic" width="400" />
5. Confirm the withdrawal transaction in MetaMask.
6. Wait a few moments to see the success message. The rewards will be released to you in 1-2 days.

## Unstake a crowdloan
Once you have claimed your rewards, you can unstake (redeem) a crowdloan.
1. Having connected your wallets, check your projects at the [Liquid Crowdloan page](https://www.ankr.com/staking/liquid-crowdloan/dot/).
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/list-of-projects.jpg" alt="Check projects for unclaimed rewards" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />
2. Choose a project and click **Redeem**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-redeem.jpg" alt="Click Redeem" class="responsive-pic" width="600" />
3. Click **Redeem DOT**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/click-redeem-dot.jpg" alt="Click Redeem DOT" class="responsive-pic" width="450" />
   <Callout type="tip">
    If you have unclaimed rewards, [claim them first](#claim-rewards) or you will lose them when redeeming.
   </Callout>
4. Choose an address from the drop-down list, or click **Send to external wallet** and enter any other Polkadot-compatible address. Then click **Redeem**.
   <img src="/docs/staking/liquid-crowdloan/enter-address-to-release-dot.jpg" alt="Enter address and click Redeem" class="responsive-pic" width="450" />
5. Confirm the redeeming transaction in MetaMask.
6. Wait a few moments to see the success message. The unstaked DOT will be released to you in 1â€“2 days.
